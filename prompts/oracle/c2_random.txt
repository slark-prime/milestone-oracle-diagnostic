# Condition C2-random — parent problem + milestones from a DIFFERENT family

Matches the C2-descriptions shape exactly, but the descriptions come from an
unrelated parent problem (same count, randomly sampled to ensure no structural
overlap). Tests whether C2-description lift depends on alignment or is a
generic prompt-scaffolding effect.

Template identical to c2_descriptions.txt, with `milestones` replaced by a
random other-family's milestones.
