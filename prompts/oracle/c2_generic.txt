# Condition C2-generic — parent problem + GENERIC "break into steps" boilerplate

Template:
    {parent_prompt}

    Hint — to solve this problem, consider the following approach:
    1. Identify the key mathematical concepts and relationships involved.
    2. Break the problem into smaller, manageable sub-steps.
    3. Solve each sub-step carefully, checking your work.
    4. Combine the results to reach the final answer.

This tests whether any kind of structured prompting (without problem-specific
content) is enough to produce lift. If C2-descriptions lifts above C2-generic,
the effect is tied to aligned content rather than generic scaffolding.
