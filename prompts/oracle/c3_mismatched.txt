# Condition C3-mismatched — correct descriptions, WRONG answers

Matches C3-gold in shape, but the answers come from mismatched (unrelated)
sub-goals. Tests that C3-gold lift depends on correct alignment, not just
the presence of answer-shaped strings.

Template (plain Python f-string):
    {parent_prompt}

    The following sub-goals have been solved:
    Sub-goal 1: {milestones[0].description}
      Answer: {wrong_answers[0]}

    Sub-goal 2: {milestones[1].description}
      Answer: {wrong_answers[1]}
    ...

    Use these results to solve the original problem.
